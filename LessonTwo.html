Strawberry Mojito

This easy recipe for a Strawberry Mojito is perfect to sip on to keep you refreshed in the summer sun.

Ingredients

  • 8-9 Strawberries
  • 600ml Sparkling water
  • 2 Limes chopped
  • 2 Sprigs of Mint leaves
  • Ice cubes
  • 100ml Strawberry syrup
  • 2 tbsp of granulated sugar

Method

  1. Combine ingredients in shaker in the order listed, except sparkling beverage.
  2. Cap and shake vigorously.
  3. Pour into serving glass and add any needed ice.
  4. Top with sparkling beverage and garnish.
